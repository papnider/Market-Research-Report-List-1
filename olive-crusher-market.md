<p><p>The olive crusher market is typically categorized into two types: single grid crushers and double grid crushers. Single grid crushers utilize a single grid to break down olives, offering a cost-effective solution for smaller operations. In contrast, double grid crushers feature two grids that enhance the crushing process, providing a more efficient extraction of oil and solids. This makes double grid crushers suitable for larger producers seeking higher throughput and quality in olive oil extraction.</p></p>
