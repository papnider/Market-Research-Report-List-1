<p><strong>The Solar Microinverter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grid-Connected Solar Microinverter</li><li>Grid-Off Solar Microinverter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar microinverter market is primarily divided into two types: grid-connected and grid-off microinverters. Grid-connected solar microinverters operate in tandem with the electric grid, converting direct current from solar panels into alternating current for immediate use or to feed back into the grid. In contrast, grid-off microinverters function independently, often used in remote areas where grid access is unavailable. These systems store energy for off-grid applications, providing reliable power solutions regardless of grid connectivity.</p></p>
