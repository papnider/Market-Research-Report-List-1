<p><ul><li>SPECT</li><li>PET</li><li>D-SPECT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ECT scanners, or Emission Computed Tomography scanners, encompass three primary market types: SPECT (Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography), PET (Positron Emission Tomography), and D-SPECT (Dynamic SPECT). SPECT utilizes gamma rays to visualize blood flow and detect abnormalities in organs, while PET provides metabolic data through radioactive tracers, highlighting cellular activity. D-SPECT enhances traditional SPECT by allowing for dynamic imaging, capturing detailed real-time physiological processes. Together, these technologies offer crucial insights into various medical conditions, aiding in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
