<p><p>The Magnesium Sulfate market is primarily divided into two types: Anhydrous Magnesium Sulfate and Hydrous Magnesium Sulfate. Anhydrous Magnesium Sulfate is a dry, granular form used in agriculture as a fertilizer and in various industrial applications due to its high purity and low moisture content. In contrast, Hydrous Magnesium Sulfate contains water molecules, making it useful in medicinal applications, as a drying agent, and in various chemical processes. Both types cater to distinct industries, driving their demand.</p></p>
