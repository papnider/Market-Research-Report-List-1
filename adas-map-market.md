<p><p>ADAS maps are crucial for advanced driver-assistance systems, and they come in two primary market types: cloud-based and embedded. Cloud-based maps leverage real-time data and extensive storage to provide dynamic updates, enhancing navigation and safety features. In contrast, embedded maps are pre-installed in the vehicle’s system, offering reliable performance without dependence on internet connectivity. This dual approach allows manufacturers to cater to diverse consumer needs, balancing real-time data access with offline capabilities for seamless driving experiences.</p></p>
