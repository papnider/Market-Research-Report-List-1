<p><p>The Low Power Single Frequency Solid State Laser market can be categorized into two main types: Pulse Type and Continuous Wave (CW) Type. Pulse Type lasers produce short bursts of energy, ideal for high precision applications such as material processing and spectroscopy, offering high peak power. In contrast, CW Type lasers emit a continuous steady beam, suitable for applications like telecommunications and laser imaging, providing consistent energy output with lower peak power, catering to diverse industrial and scientific needs.</p></p>
