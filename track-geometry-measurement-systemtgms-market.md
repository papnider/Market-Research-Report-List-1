<p><p>Track Geometry Measurement Systems (TGMS) are essential for assessing railway track conditions, ensuring safety and efficiency. The market is divided into two main types: </p><p>1. **No Contact Based TGMS** utilizes technologies like lasers or GNSS for remote measurements, ensuring minimal disruption to rail operations. </p><p>2. **Contact Based TGMS** involves physical contact measurement devices, such as track recording cars or portable gauges, providing high accuracy but requiring track access during surveys. Both types play crucial roles in maintaining rail infrastructure.</p></p>
