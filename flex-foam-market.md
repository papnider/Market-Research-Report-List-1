<p><ul><li>Closed Cell</li><li>Open Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Flex Foam market is segmented into two primary types: closed cell and open cell foam. Closed cell foam is characterized by its sealed cells, offering enhanced insulation, water resistance, and durability, making it ideal for applications in construction and automotive sectors. In contrast, open cell foam features interconnected cells, providing superior breathability and cushioning, suitable for furniture, bedding, and soundproofing. Both types cater to diverse industries, contributing to their growing demand based on specific performance requirements.</p></p>
