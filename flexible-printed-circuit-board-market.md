<p><p>The Flexible Printed Circuit Board (FPCB) market primarily consists of single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided FPCBs feature conductive traces on one side, making them simpler and cost-effective for basic applications. In contrast, double-sided FPCBs have conductive paths on both sides, allowing for greater circuit complexity and higher density connections. This dual-sided design accommodates more components and enhances functionality, making double-sided FPCBs suitable for advanced electronic devices such as smartphones and medical equipment. Both types cater to diverse industry demands.</p></p>
