<p><strong>The Neuro Monitoring Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electroencephalogram</li><li>Electromyography</li><li>Transcranial Doppler</li><li>Intracranial Pressure Monitor</li><li>Cerebral Oximeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Neuro monitoring devices encompass various technologies for assessing brain and nervous system functions. Electroencephalograms (EEGs) track electrical activity in the brain, while electromyography (EMG) measures muscle response to nervous stimulation. Transcranial Doppler employs ultrasound to evaluate cerebral blood flow, whereas intracranial pressure monitors assess pressure within the skull, crucial for detecting conditions like traumatic brain injury. Cerebral oximeters measure oxygen levels in brain tissue, aiding in the monitoring of neurological health and guiding treatment decisions.</p></p>
