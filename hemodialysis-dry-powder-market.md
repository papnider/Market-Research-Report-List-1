<p><p>The hemodialysis dry powder market comprises primarily two types: acetic acid type dialysis powder and citric acid type dialysis powder. Acetic acid type is used for its effectiveness in maintaining acid-base balance during dialysis and is often favored for its cost-effectiveness. In contrast, citric acid type dialysis powder serves to regulate citrate levels, providing an alternative that may enhance the treatment of specific conditions. Both types play crucial roles in improving patient outcomes in hemodialysis treatments.</p></p>
