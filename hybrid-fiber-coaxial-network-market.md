<p><p>The Hybrid Fiber Coaxial (HFC) network market is categorized into DOCSIS 3.0 and below, and DOCSIS 3.1. DOCSIS 3.0 and below support lower data speeds, catering to basic broadband needs, while enabling multiple services over a single network. In contrast, DOCSIS 3.1 significantly enhances capacity, allowing higher data rates and improved network efficiency.
