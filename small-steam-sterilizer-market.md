<p><strong>The Small Steam Sterilizer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity</li><li>SFPP</li><li>Pre-Vac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small steam sterilizer market includes various types designed for different sterilization processes. Gravity displacement sterilizers use gravity to allow steam to displace air, ensuring effective sterilization. Steam-flush pressure-pulse (SFPP) sterilizers utilize a combination of steam flushing and pressure pulses to enhance air removal and improve efficiency. Pre-vacuum sterilizers employ a vacuum to eliminate air before steam introduction, ensuring more effective penetration for sterilizing delicate instruments. Each type caters to specific needs in healthcare and laboratory settings.</p></p>
