<p><p>The Pregnancy Point of Care Testing market includes various test types that facilitate quick and accurate detection of pregnancy-related hormones. LH Urine Tests measure luteinizing hormone levels, indicating ovulation. FSH Urine Tests assess follicle-stimulating hormone for fertility monitoring. HCG Blood Tests provide precise quantification of human chorionic gonadotropin, confirming pregnancy earlier and more accurately than urine tests. HCG Urine Tests offer a convenient, non-invasive option for detecting pregnancy through the presence of hCG hormones, commonly used for home pregnancy testing.</p></p>
