<p><ul><li>Capacitive Screen Touch Screen Pen</li><li>Resistive Screen Touch Screen Pen</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The touch screen pen market is divided into two main types: capacitive and resistive screen touch screen pens. Capacitive screen touch pens utilize conductive materials to interact with the capacitive screens commonly found in smartphones and tablets, providing greater precision and responsiveness. In contrast, resistive screen touch pens apply pressure to the screen to register a touch, which makes them suitable for older devices often used in industrial or medical applications. Each type serves specific user needs and device compatibility.</p></p>
