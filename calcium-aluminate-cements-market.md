<p><p>Calcium aluminate cements (CACs) are categorized based on their alumina content, with key types including CA-50, CA-70, CA-80, and others. CA-50 contains about 50% alumina, offering moderate early strength and setting time, making it suitable for general applications. CA-70 has 70% alumina, providing higher strength and resistance to aggressive environments, ideal for industrial uses. CA-80 features approximately 80% alumina, ensuring rapid hardening and exceptional durability for specialized applications. Other types may include variations and blends tailored for unique performance requirements.</p></p>
