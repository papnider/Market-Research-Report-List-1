<p><p>The fishing nets market is primarily divided into knotted and knotless nets, each serving specific needs in the fishing industry. Knotted nets are traditional, offering durability and strength, making them ideal for various fishing techniques. They are commonly used in commercial and recreational fishing. These nets are increasingly popular for delicate species and in environments requiring minimal bycatch.</p></p>
