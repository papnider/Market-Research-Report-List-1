<p><strong>The Photosensitive Drum Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Photosensitive Drum</li><li>Non-Integrated Photosensitive Drum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photosensitive drum market is primarily divided into two types: integrated and non-integrated photosensitive drums. Integrated photosensitive drums combine the imaging drum and the developer unit, optimizing space and simplifying maintenance, often used in compact printers. Non-integrated drums separate the imaging drum from the developer, allowing for more versatility in replacement and higher quality printing, commonly found in professional-grade printers. Each type serves different user needs, reflecting distinct performance characteristics and operational efficiencies in printing applications.</p></p>
