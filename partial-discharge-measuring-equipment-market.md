<p><p>The Partial Discharge Measuring Equipment Market is segmented based on types of discharges: Internal Discharges, Surface Discharges, and Corona Discharges. Internal Discharges occur within insulation systems and can indicate degradation, while Surface Discharges happen on the insulation surface and can lead to failure due to contaminants. Corona Discharges are localized ionization phenomena around conductors, often causing high-frequency noise. Each type requires specific measuring techniques for effective monitoring and diagnostics, vital for maintaining electrical equipment integrity and safety.</p></p>
