<p><p>The Dental Aerosol Suction Unit market is segmented into Primary and Secondary Filtration Units. Primary Filtration Units are designed to capture larger aerosol particles at the source during dental procedures, helping to reduce immediate exposure to contaminants. Secondary Filtration Units focus on refining air quality by filtering smaller particles and pathogens after initial suctioning, often incorporating HEPA filters. Together, these units enhance the safety of dental environments by minimizing airborne infection risks and providing cleaner air for both patients and practitioners.</p></p>
