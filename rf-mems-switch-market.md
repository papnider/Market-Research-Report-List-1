<p><ul><li>Single-pole</li><li>Multi-pole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF MEMS switch market is categorized into single-pole and multi-pole types. Single-pole switches control one signal path, offering simplicity and low power consumption, making them ideal for applications like antenna switching and phase shifters. Multi-pole switches, on the other hand, manage multiple signal paths simultaneously, enabling complex signal routing in advanced systems. This versatility supports applications in telecommunications, aerospace, and automotive industries, facilitating high-frequency performance and improved system integration, driving demand for both types in the market.</p></p>
