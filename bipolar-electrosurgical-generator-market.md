<p><p>A Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator is a medical device that utilizes high-frequency electrical currents to cut or coagulate tissue during surgical procedures. Unlike monopolar generators, which require grounding pads and can affect a broader area of tissue, bipolar generators deliver energy directly between two tips on the surgical instrument, allowing for more precise control and reduced thermal spread. This accuracy minimizes damage to surrounding tissues, making it particularly beneficial in delicate surgeries.</p><p>The Bipolar Electrosurgical Generator Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by technological advancements, increasing surgical procedures, and rising awareness of minimally invasive techniques.
