<p><p>The manual chromatography syringe market is primarily divided into two types: fixed and removable syringes. Fixed syringes are designed as a single unit, providing durability and minimizing contamination risk, making them suitable for precise applications. In contrast, removable syringes allow for the interchangeability of syringes and barrels, offering flexibility for different sample types and volumes. Both types cater to specific laboratory needs, with fixed syringes ideal for routine tasks, while removable ones are advantageous for diverse experimental setups.</p></p>
