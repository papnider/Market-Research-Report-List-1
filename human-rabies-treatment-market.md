<p><p>The Human Rabies Treatment Market encompasses two key applications: Pre-Exposure Immunization (PEI) and Post-Exposure Prophylaxis (PEP). PEI is administered to individuals at high risk of rabies exposure, providing preventive protection through a series of vaccinations. Both applications are crucial in controlling rabies and reducing mortality rates globally.</p></p>
