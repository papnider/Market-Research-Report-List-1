<p><p>The automotive dash camera market is divided into two main types: single-channel and multi-channel systems. Single-channel dash cameras feature one lens, primarily recording the front view of a vehicle, making them suitable for basic monitoring. In contrast, multi-channel dash cameras come with multiple lenses, capturing footage from various angles, including the front, rear, and interior of the vehicle. This expanded coverage enhances safety and accountability by providing a comprehensive view during incidents or accidents.</p></p>
