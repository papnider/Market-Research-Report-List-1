<p><p>The Monofocal Intraocular Lens (IOL) market is segmented into two main types: Not Pre-loaded IOLs and Pre-loaded IOLs. Not Pre-loaded IOLs require manual loading into an injector before implantation, offering flexibility for surgeons but demanding precise handling and technique. In contrast, Pre-loaded IOLs come pre-packaged in a delivery system, allowing for quicker and easier implantation, which can enhance operational efficiency and reduce the risk of contamination. Both types serve essential roles in cataract surgeries, catering to different surgical preferences.</p></p>
