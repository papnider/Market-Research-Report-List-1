<p><ul><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Masticating</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric juicer market is primarily divided into three types: centrifugal, masticating, and other juicers. Centrifugal juicers operate at high speeds, using a spinning blade to extract juice quickly, making them popular for their convenience. Masticating juicers, or slow juicers, extract juice by crushing and squeezing fruits and vegetables, preserving more nutrients and yielding higher juice quality. Other types include citrus juicers and triturating juicers, offering specialized functions based on user needs and preferences. Each type caters to different consumer requirements and juicing styles.</p></p>
