<p><p>The polymer stabilizer market comprises three main types: antioxidants, heat stabilizers, and light stabilizers. Antioxidants prevent degradation of polymers caused by oxidative reactions, extending product lifespan. Heat stabilizers protect against thermal decomposition during processing and end-use, ensuring material integrity at elevated temperatures. Light stabilizers shield polymers from UV radiation, reducing photo-degradation and maintaining color and mechanical properties. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing the durability and performance of various polymer applications across industries.</p></p>
