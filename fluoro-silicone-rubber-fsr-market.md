<p><p>Fluoro Silicone Rubber (FSR) is a specialized elastomer known for its excellent chemical resistance, thermal stability, and low compression set properties. The market is segmented into two main types: High Temperature Vulcanization (HTV) and Room Temperature Vulcanization (RTV). HTV FSR is cured at elevated temperatures, offering superior mechanical strength and durability, suitable for applications in extreme environments. Conversely, RTV FSR cures at room temperature, providing ease of processing and versatility for various applications, especially where lower temperature resistance is sufficient.</p></p>
