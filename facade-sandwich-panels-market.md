<p><ul><li>EPS Sandwich Panels</li><li>PUR/PIR Sandwich Panels</li><li>Mineral Wool Sandwich Panels</li><li>PF Sandwich Panels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Facade sandwich panels are constructed using multiple layers, providing insulation and structural support. EPS (Expanded Polystyrene) sandwich panels offer lightweight insulation, making them cost-effective. PUR/PIR (Polyurethane/Polyisocyanurate) panels provide superior thermal performance and fire resistance. Mineral wool sandwich panels are fire-resistant and soundproof, ideal for commercial buildings. PF (Phenolic Foam) sandwich panels offer excellent thermal insulation and low flammability, making them suitable for high-performance applications. Each type caters to specific building requirements based on insulation needs and fire safety standards.</p></p>
